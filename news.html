Landing At Vandenberg

SpaceX recently made a historic landing at VAB in California, opening new and faster ways to launch rockets from factory to launch pad. The launch also caused a twilight accross LA making a firework show for the city. The payload was SAOCOM a satellite for an Argentinian based company.

Crew Dragon Mission

Later next year SpaceX will be the first ever private company to send astronauts to the ISS and will also be the first American rocket, to launch American astronauts from American soil to the ISS since the space shuttle retired in 2011. The Dragon spacecraft will take the astronauts to the ISS on the Falcon 9. Making the need for the Soyuz and the Russian alliance with NASA no longer needed.

Soyuz Failure

On Thursday Oct 11, the Russian Soyuz rocket had its third ever failure while carrying two astronauts to the ISS. After a booster rocket failed the crew had the make an escape via a ballistic landing (landing at a sharper angle). This means that the current astronauts on the ISS are marooned until a new Soyuz can make it up or SpaceX or Boeing can make it up, the next supply mission from SpaceX won't be until November.
