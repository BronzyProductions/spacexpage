SpaceX

SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

History & Achievement

"Failure is an option here. If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough."-Elon Musk/CEO

SpaceX was set back by many failures in its early days of exploration but has rose like a Phoenix from the Falcon 1's ashes and began to explore deeper. Developing their Falcon 1 rocket just managing to get a successful launch from the last parts they had. Being awarded the ISS contract missions with NASA and being the first private company to dock with the ISS. Later developing many models of their historic Falcon 9 rocket, most recent being the Block 5 upgrade. And then February last year launching their Falcon Heavy rocket to amaze the world with its power. SpaceX signed private passengers for the BFR and are set to send astronauts to the ISS in a shared contract with Boeing early next year. All of this and many more milestones to come. Ad Astra!

2018

Falcon Heavy's maiden flight, launching a cherry red 2008 Tesla Roadster into and orbit around the sun.

2017

First ever re-usable Dragon craft used and first ever re-flight of the Falcon 9 orbital class booster.

2016

First ever landing on the drone ship. Drone ship "Of Course I Still Love You" was the landing platform for the Falcon 9 returning from launching the dragon into orbit.

2015

On a historic day, SpaceX became the first ever company to land an obital class rocket. After launching a communications sattelite the Falcon 9 landed back at Cape Canaveral. SpaceX also began testing a launch abort system for their passenger Dragon V2. SpaceX also started playing with the idea of a first stage drone ship landing.

2014

SpaceX awarded $2.6B commercial crew contract for Dragon V2 from NASA. The "Grasshopper" began testing the feasibility of a first stage landing and the first stage of the Falcon 9 makes its first soft water landing in the Atlantic Ocean.

2013

First flight of Dragon to GTO, Grasshopper lander vehicle makes half a mile flight.

2012

Dragon visits Space Station, being the first ever private company to visit the ISS.

2010

Dragon becomes the first ever privately developed spacecraft to return from low earth orbit. Falcon 9 makes first successful flight, meeting all mission objectives.

2009

Falcon 1 Flight 5 makes history, becoming the first privately developed liquid fuel rocket to deliver a commercial satellite to Earth orbit

2008

NASA awards SpaceX $1.6B Commercial Resupply Services (CRS) contract. Falcon 1 becomes the first privately developed liquid fuel rocket to reach Earth orbit....YASSSSSSSS
