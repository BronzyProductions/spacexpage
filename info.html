SpaceX

SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

History & Achievement

"Failure is an option here. If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough."-Elon Musk/CEO

SpaceX was set back by many failures in its early days of exploration but has rose like a Phoenix from the Falcon 1's ashes and began to explore deeper. Developing their Falcon 1 rocket just managing to get a successful launch from the last parts they had. Being awarded the ISS contract missions with NASA and being the first private company to dock with the ISS. Later developing many models of their historic Falcon 9 rocket, most recent being the Block 5 upgrade. And then February last year launching their Falcon Heavy rocket to amaze the world with its power. SpaceX signed private passengers for the BFR and are set to send astronauts to the ISS in a shared contract with Boeing early next year. All of this and many more milestones to come. Ad Astra!
